Your chatbot will be accessed through a Facebook page. For this reason, it is important that you create a page for your company on Facebook. If your company has a page already, you may use it.

To connect your chatbot to Facebook, you need to use an App.

Use my own App

To connect your personal App, you'll need your App's Id and Secret. If you don't know how to create an App on Facebook, or need help creating a Page Access Token, check out this article in Help Center

Use iMakerBot native App

Please enter your Facebook account to proceed.

To use the customer chat plugin, you must:

. Whitelist the domain of your website
. Include the Facebook JavaScript SDK in the page
. Include the plugin on your webpage
